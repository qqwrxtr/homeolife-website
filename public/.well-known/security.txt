# Security Policy for HomeoLife Ukraine Medical Website

## Scope
This security policy covers:
- Main website: homeolife.com.ua
- All subdomains
- Patient data systems
- Medical consultation platforms
- Payment processing systems

## Priority Issues
1. Patient data exposure
2. Medical record breaches
3. Payment system vulnerabilities
4. Authentication bypasses
5. Session management flaws

## Reporting Guidelines
- Response time: 48 hours maximum
- Severity assessment within 72 hours
- Fix deployment: Critical issues within 7 days
- Disclosure timeline: 90 days coordinated disclosure

## Rewards
We recognize security researchers who help us maintain patient safety:
- Critical vulnerabilities: Recognition + Thank you letter
- High severity: Hall of Fame listing
- Medium/Low: Security acknowledgment

## Out of Scope
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- DoS/DDoS attacks
- Spam or phishing
- Issues requiring unusual user interaction

## Legal Safe Harbor
We commit to:
- Not pursue legal action for good faith security research
- Work with researchers to understand and fix issues
- Recognize contributions publicly (with permission)

## Medical Data Protection
Special consideration for:
- HIPAA-equivalent protections
- GDPR compliance for EU patients
- Ukrainian medical data regulations
- Patient confidentiality requirements
